Our Story

Founded in 2015, Spice & Slice began as a small family restaurant with a simple idea - combining the best of Indian flavors with the universal love for pizza.

Our founder, Chef Rajesh Patel, spent years perfecting the recipes, ensuring each pizza offers an authentic taste of India while maintaining the essence of traditional pizza.

Today, we have 12 locations across India and have served over 1 million happy customers. Our mission remains the same - to deliver innovative, flavor-packed pizzas that celebrate India's rich culinary heritage.
